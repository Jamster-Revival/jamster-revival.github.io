Nessie's Fire Bubbles

Game Type: Java

Description: Welcome to the volcanic Island of Dragonia!
Until a few days ago Dragonia seemed a peaceful, perfect home for the little green dragon living on this island. Suddenly, BANG! The big volcano spits out coloured fire bubbles ... and that is not a good sign! There is only one chance: help the little green dragon to drop the fire bubbles in the right mini volcano! And with a little luck the big volcano will become quiet and everything will be peaceful again. "Bubbles" requires skill, the right tactics and most of all a cool head to achieve increasingly difficult combos and high scores.
